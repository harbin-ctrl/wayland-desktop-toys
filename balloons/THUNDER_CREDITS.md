# Thunder

Thunder is generated procedurally in `audio.c` at application startup. It does
not include, download, or derive from an external recording.

Its envelope and frequency balance were characterized against CC0 field
recordings used only as listening and measurement references:

- “Distant Thunder.wav” by sarson, recorded without rain:
  <https://freesound.org/s/195522/>
- “Distant Thunder 1” by Fission9:
  <https://freesound.org/s/581122/>
- “Extremely distant thunder” by supercell10:
  <https://freesound.org/s/393634/>
